Major Schedule Change for Wednesday

8th September 2010 by Steve Holden

Who on Earth is coming to DjangoCon at 5:15 this afternoon?

Unfortunately, that has to be a surprise - nothing is definite, yet, but we are hoping to receive a short talk from a surprise guest at 5:15 today.

In order to accommodate this extra 15 minutes into an already-busy program we are shortening the lunch period by 15 minutes, and so will restart at 1:15 rather than 1:30. This means the whole afternoon has to run 15 minutes ahead. Being geeks, I am sure you can cope.

I hope this special treat actually comes off, but this is not a promise. If not, we will simply start the DjangoConversazione a little early.
