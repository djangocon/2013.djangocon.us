Speaker Profile

Peter Baumgartner

Peter Baumgartner founded Lincoln Loop, one of the first companies to focus on providing Django development and consulting services, in 2007. The small agency claims companies such as National Geographic, PBS, Nasuni, and Redbeacon among its client list.

When not working, Peter enjoys the outdoors (skiing, biking, kayaking) and spending time with his wife and 2 children.
